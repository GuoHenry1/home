ACTION!

Action anime is exactly as it says. It's anime with action in it. Some of the more famous action anime are,

  • Sword Art Online
  • Attack on Titan
  • Naruto

Out of all the animes out there the most used tag would be the action genre. It is included in every mainstream anime and targets a teenage male audience. Action animes generally revolve around fighting with an end goal in mind. Attack on Titan for example is a fight for survival. The main character fights to save others and more importantly to exterminate Titans forever.

ADVENTURE!

Adventure is also an extremely popular type of anime. It is also a tag that is very popular among mainstream anime such as

  • One Piece
  • Fullmetal Alchemist
  • No Game No Life

Adventure anime involves a quest or something resembles one. Characters in adventure anime hold the properties of a hero. They always win and will always make it through what ever issue they are in. No Game No Life is a great example of it. 2 likable characters are brought to another world where everything revolves around games and especially winning them. The main cahracters are conviently the best gamers in the world which results in quite an interesting series.
