Romance!

Romance anime, the heartbreakers, the tearjerkers of the the world.

  • Shigatsu wa Kimi no Uso
  • Toradora
  • Clannad

Romance animes are generally set in high school. They involve a slow plot with a bit of comedy to make it more entertaining. The best part about these ones are the endings, they make you feel a mix of emotions and are one of the reasons that this genre is popular. Your Lie in April for example has a heartbreaking ending involving a note of one of the dead characters.

Sci-fi!

Science fiction anime are for the braniacs in the community, largely involving space or some futuristic theme.

  • Steins Gate
  • Code Geass
  • Neon Genesis Evangelion

Science fiction can be very intriguing at times and is very interesting to think about much like fantasy. Science fiction stories make you think alot and for those people that enjoy brain teasers, science fiction is perfect for you. Steins Gate is an incredible anime about different world lines and has a great plot to go along with it. It is one of the most popular animes of all time!
